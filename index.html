About Us

Nosotros

A Family Tradition, Brought to Lanzarote

Una Tradición Familiar, Traída a Lanzarote

Hippie Chippy is a family-run business with many years of experience behind us — built on the foundations of our UK-based restaurant. Our chef and owner was raised in the UK serving fish & chips in a family-owned business, learning the craft the proper way: fresh fish, batter made by hand, and chips cut from whole potatoes.

Hippie Chippy es un negocio familiar con muchos años de experiencia — construido sobre las bases de nuestro restaurante en el Reino Unido. Nuestro chef y propietario se crió en el Reino Unido sirviendo fish & chips en un negocio familiar, aprendiendo el oficio de la forma correcta: pescado fresco, rebozado hecho a mano y patatas cortadas a mano.

"Best fish and chips I've had — the batter was light and just dissolves in your mouth. I don't know how they do it."
— Terry P, Leeds

Now that same passion and family recipe has crossed the Atlantic to Costa Teguise, Lanzarote. We serve everyone from holidaymakers craving a real taste of home to local residents who've made us their regular. Every portion is cooked fresh, every time — no frozen fish, no compromises. Just proper British fish & chips, made with love.
